Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a tenant?

As a tenant you need to register with the land lord or ask his permission if you can rent or if there is still a vacancy.

What should I do if I encounter a maintenance issue?

If you encounter an issue with staying, you can ask your land lord or say that there are issues with your roommate or your room.

How do I make a payment for my room?

In order to pay for your room, you can advance or down a few payments and then wait a month before paying again.

Can I switch rooms during my stay?

You can also move during your stay if you have a problem with your room or your roommate because you can't study or fucos with your neighbor because they are noisy.

What happens if I miss a payment?

They can also evict you if you reach 5 months late in payment or it depends on your land lord if you don't have money you can also talk to your land lord and you can pay this month.
